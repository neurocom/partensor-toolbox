Computes the "Partial" Khatri-Rao product and the Matricized Tensor Khatri-Rao Product (MTTKRP). More specifically, computes the Khatri-Rao product for all factors apart from factors[mode] and use it to finally calculate the MTTKRP.

Template Parameters
_TnsSizeSize of the factors array.
Parameters
factors[in] An stl array with the factors.
tns_mat[in] Matricization of the Tensor based on mode.
mode[in] The dimension where the tensor was matricized and the MTTKRP will be computed.
krao[in,out] The result Khatri-Rao product for the factors excluding the mode factor.
result[in/out] The result matrix of the multiplication of the matricized tensor and the Khatri-Rao product.
